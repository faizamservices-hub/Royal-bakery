Our Craft

Baked Before Sunrise, Gone by Sundown.

Every morning begins at 4 a.m., when our bakers fold butter into dough by hand and let nothing rush the process. We use single-origin chocolate, real Madagascar vanilla, and butter that's never seen a substitute. Nothing here is mass-produced — if a tray sells out, the next batch simply takes longer, and we think that's exactly how it should be.

What you taste is the difference between a bakery and a kitchen that happens to sell pastries. We're the latter, on purpose.
